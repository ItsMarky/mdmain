FAQs & Technical Specifications (Web)

Answers to common questions and details on the technology stack used for managed services.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

  • How long does a new static site take to build? Typically 2-4 weeks, depending on the complexity and how quickly content is provided.
  • What is the difference between static and managed hosting? All sites are hosted on high-performance cloud servers. **Static hosting** is optimized for speed and simplicity, suitable for brochure sites. **Managed hosting** is required when using dynamic features like online shops or blogs (CMS), as it includes the necessary server environment, security patching, and updates.
  • What if I want to switch to a CMS later? I can seamlessly upgrade your hosting package and migrate your existing design to a platform like WordPress.

Technical Specifications for Managed Hosting

  • CMS Platforms: Primarily WordPress, but can accommodate other PHP-based systems on request.
  • Server Environment: High-performance, managed VPS/Cloud infrastructure, optimized for speed and resilience.
  • E-Commerce Integration: Managed WooCommerce or Shopify setup, supporting all major payment gateways (Stripe, PayPal, etc.).
  • Email and Productivity: Professional business email (IMAP/POP3) included. Optional integration and license management for Microsoft 365 (M365).

Safety, FAQs & Technical Specifications (Drone)

Answers to common questions and details about our CAA compliance, safety procedures, and equipment.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

  • What happens if the weather is bad? Safety is paramount. If wind, rain, or low visibility prevents the flight, we offer a free, no-penalty reschedule at a time convenient for you.
  • Do you handle permits and airspace checks? Yes. As a CAA A2 qualified pilot, I manage all necessary pre-flight risk assessments, airspace checks, and regulatory permissions for every commercial job.
  • How long until I receive the final media? Raw footage is often available immediately. Professionally edited photos and video are delivered securely via cloud link within 3-5 business days.

Technical & Operational Specifications

  • Camera & Video Quality: High-spec, lightweight drone technology capable of **4K video at 60fps** and **50MP stills**, utilizing professional **10-bit colour profiles** (D-Log M) for maximum editing flexibility.
  • Operations & Compliance: Use of lightweight, registered drone technology allows for operations with enhanced safety standards and increased proximity permissions compared to heavier commercial drones.
  • Licensing & Insurance: I hold **CAA A2 CofC** qualification and maintain comprehensive Public Liability Insurance for all commercial operations.
  • Deliverables: Final media is provided in high-quality formats (H.265, JPEG/RAW) via a private, secure download link.
